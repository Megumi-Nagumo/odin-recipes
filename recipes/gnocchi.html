Gnocchi alla Sorrentina

Ingredients

  • 1 pound potato gnocchi
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella cheese, diced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Cook the gnocchi according to package instructions until they float to the surface.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat and add the marinara sauce.
  4. Once the sauce is heated, add the cooked gnocchi and stir gently to coat.
  5. Transfer the gnocchi to a baking dish and top with diced mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  7. Remove from the oven, garnish with fresh basil leaves, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your Gnocchi alla Sorrentina!

Note: You can also add cooked vegetables or meat to the sauce for additional flavor and texture.

Pair with a side salad or garlic bread for a complete meal.

Affogato

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brewed espresso or strong coffee
  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • Chocolate shavings or cocoa powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine espresso and vanilla ice cream in a blender or food processor.
  2. Add chocolate shavings or cocoa powder, if desired.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Serve immediately or refrigerate for a few hours before serving.

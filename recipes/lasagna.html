Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with half of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking pan.
  6. Layer lasagna noodles, then meat sauce, then cheese mixture.
  7. Repeat layers, and top with remaining cheese mixture.

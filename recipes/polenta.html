Beef Stew with Polenta

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef chuck, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup polenta
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, brown the beef cubes over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, carrots, celery, and garlic; saut√© until vegetables are tender.
  3. Pour in beef broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours until beef is tender.
  5. Meanwhile, cook polenta according to package instructions.
  6. Serve the beef stew over a bed of creamy polenta.
